Your body is not weak 🌸

Periods are a time for rest, softness, and self-compassion.

💗 Emotional Changes During Periods

Mood swings, sadness, anger, or increased desire are completely normal. Hormones fluctuate and your emotions become more sensitive — it does NOT mean something is wrong with you.

🌿 Gentle Period Self-Care

  • Warm showers or heating pads
  • Light stretching or rest
  • Comfort foods & hydration
  • Loose clothing & deep breathing

❌ Things You Don’t Need to Feel Ashamed Of

Crying, needing space, feeling tired, or wanting comfort. Your body is doing hard work.

Period Care & Comfort 🌸

Periods are natural. Your comfort, hygiene, and mental peace matter. This space is here to support you — gently and honestly.

Myths vs Facts

  • Myth: Periods are dirty
    Fact: Periods are a healthy body function
  • Myth: You shouldn’t bath
    Fact: Bathing helps hygiene & comfort
  • Myth: Pain is always normal
    Fact: Severe pain should be checked

✖ Avoid scented products
